Does White Claw® Hard Seltzer contain allergens?
Our products don’t contain common allergens like milk, eggs, fish, crustacean shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat and soybeans. Even though White Claw® Hard Seltzer’s full ingredient list is proprietary, if you’re concerned about an ingredient, we will gladly let you know if our products contain any.
Is White Claw® Hard Seltzer gluten free?
Yes! White Claw‌® Hard Seltzer is made with naturally gluten-free ingredients.
